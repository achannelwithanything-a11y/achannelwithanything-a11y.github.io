What is Mental Health?

Mental health is about how we think, feel, and act. It affects how we handle stress, relate to others, and make choices every day.

Just like physical health, everyone has mental health — and it’s important to look after it.

Why It Matters

Good mental health helps you focus in school, build friendships, and enjoy life. If you’re feeling sad, anxious, or stressed, you’re not alone — and help is always available.
